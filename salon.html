Salon

When you walk through our doors, you enter a place where beauty and community come together. Current Salon & Color Bar is more than a salon — it's a culture, a creative hub, and a welcoming space for every client.

Located in beautiful One Loudoun in Ashburn, VA, our modern, thoughtfully designed space is the backdrop for expert hair care, meaningful connections, and results that speak for themselves.

Beauty Through Artistry

As the First & Only MOB Certified Diamond Salon in the World, we hold ourselves to an extraordinary standard. Every stylist on our team brings passion, precision, and an artist's eye to each appointment.

MOB Certified Diamond status represents the pinnacle of salon excellence — from education and technical mastery to client experience and business integrity. We are proud to be in a class of our own.
