About Nese

Nese Altas is the creative force and master colorist behind Current Salon & Color Bar. With over two decades of experience in the beauty industry, she has built a reputation as one of Northern Virginia's most sought-after stylists — known for her extraordinary precision, artistry, and ability to make every client feel truly seen.

Born with an innate eye for color and detail, Nese trained under some of the industry's most respected mentors before going on to earn a long list of accolades — including MOB Certified Diamond Salon recognition, a distinction held by fewer than one percent of salons in the country.

As an Apprenticeship Instructor, Nese is deeply committed to elevating the next generation of stylists. She mentors junior team members with the same passion and precision she brings to every client — sharing her techniques, her standards, and her belief that every haircut and color service is an act of care.

When you sit in Nese's chair, you're not just getting a service. You're getting two decades of mastery, a thoughtful consultation, and a result you'll love for weeks to come.
