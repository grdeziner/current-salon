Team

Get to Know Us

Our team is a carefully curated group of talented stylists, colorists, and beauty professionals who share a common passion: making every client look and feel extraordinary.

Led by award-winning stylist and owner Nese Altas, each team member is committed to ongoing education, technical mastery, and creative artistry. Our talent tier is based on years of experience, continuing education, skills, and creative vision.

Whether you're looking for a fresh cut, a bold color transformation, or simply a relaxing experience, you'll find a stylist here who is perfectly matched to your needs.
