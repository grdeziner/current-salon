Texture & Treatment

Take control of your mornings. Our Keratin and texture smoothing treatments dramatically reduce frizz, cut blow-dry time in half, and leave your hair with a healthy, mirror-like shine that lasts for months.

Whether you're battling humidity in Virginia summers or simply want hair that cooperates, our texture specialists will match you with the right treatment — from a gentle smoothing system to a full Keratin straightening treatment. We also offer curl definition services for clients who want to enhance their natural texture.
